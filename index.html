TIP 4 TEENS
2019 Technical Inclusion Program

The Midtown Techical
Inclusion Program

TIP (Technical Inclusion Project) for teens is a project focusing on the 12 to 18-year old demographic for the underrepresented communities in Midtown of Detroit, MI.

The Technical Inclusion Project focuses on hands-on experience with technology that will initially introduce teens to the digital world through the essentials of application development that would lead to building websites and apps.

It is our expectation that sufficient interest in technology will be generated by providing the necessary tools for entering the digital world through web design and creating an appetite for pursuing more advanced emerging technologies.

The scope of Technical Inclusion will include basic programming concepts, internet foundations, web, and mobile app basics, design, development, and publishing.
